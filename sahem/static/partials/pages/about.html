About Sahem

Sahem is an open social platform for creating and managing event requirements.

Sahem (سَاهمْ) means Share, Contribute and Join - Almaani dictionary

It's open because it's for everyone, we're trying to make it free, easy to use and accessible for screen readers.
And because it's open source, you can contribute as a developer to make it more awsome !

We called it VERY SOCIAL NETWORK because we make it so you can meet people and organize real life social events.
